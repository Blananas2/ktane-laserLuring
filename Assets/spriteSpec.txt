Cat Sprites:
    Number is via the following formula
    (10xC)+A
    where C is the Cat:
        0 = Cleo | orange
        1 = Dart | black   (mostly undrawn)
        2 = Finn | white   〃
		3 = Lena | persian 〃
		4 = Nuki | calico  〃
		5 = Remi | tabby   〃
		6 = Scar | siamese 〃
		7 = Tiki | tux     〃
		8 = Vivi | jellie  〃
		9 = Wind | gray    〃
    where A is the Action:
        0 = Sitting
        1-8 = Walk Cycle [ascending frame order]
        9 = Pouncing (undrawn)
{May adjust formula later; will likely add more breeds}
